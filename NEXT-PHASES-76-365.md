# Mapato — Next Phases: Days 76-365

## Phase 8: Scale & Ecosystem (Days 201–365)

**Theme:** *The platform becomes the marketplace*

### Goal
Transform Mapato into an ecosystem — partner marketplace, white-label offering, trade finance bridge, and AfCFTA readiness hub. Hit $100K+ MRR and establish Mapato as the leading B2B trade automation platform in Africa.

### Milestones

| Day | Milestone | Deliverable |
|-----|-----------|-------------|
| 220 | Partner Marketplace launch | 50+ certified automation agencies reselling/whitelabelling Mapato |
| 240 | White-label offering | Agencies deploy Mapato under their own brand with custom pricing |
| 260 | Trade Finance Bridge | Mapato integrates directly with AfDB/AFAWA, Sokogate Pay, letter of credit providers |
| 290 | AfCFTA Readiness Hub | Automated compliance dashboard for continental trade under African Continental Free Trade Area |
| 330 | West Africa expansion | Nigeria, Ghana, Senegal market entry |
| 365 | Year 1 review | $100K+ MRR, 100+ active clients, 10+ countries, $1M+ ARR |

### Ecosystem Components

#### 1. Partner Marketplace
- 50+ certified automation agencies across Africa
- Reseller tiers: Bronze ($1K/mo), Silver ($3K/mo), Gold ($10K/mo)
- White-label option: Agencies deploy Mapato as their own product
- Revenue share: 70/30 in favor of partners (year 1), 60/40 (year 2+)
- Central support hub: Training, marketing materials, sales enablement

#### 2. White-Label Platform
- Full rebranding: Custom domain, logo, colors, messaging
- Custom pricing: Partners set their own pricing tiers
- Isolated instance: Partner's clients never see "Mapato" branding
- Managed infrastructure: Mapato handles hosting, security, updates

#### 3. Trade Finance Bridge
- **AfDB/AFAWA**: Direct application for women-led SME trade finance
- **Sokogate Pay**: Escrow services for cross-border transactions
- **Letters of Credit**: Automated LC application via partner banks
- **Credit scoring**: Mapato ERS score used as creditworthiness indicator

#### 4. AfCFTA Readiness Hub
- Automated compliance dashboard for African Continental Free Trade Area
- Tariff calculator: Instant duty/tariff estimates for intra-African trade
- Rules of origin checker: Verify products qualify for preferential treatment
- Documentation generator: Auto-fill certificates of origin, export declarations

#### 5. Korean Corporate Corridor (Days 140–365)
- **Target**: 10 Korean mid-sized manufacturers & chaebol procurement teams seeking supply chain diversification
- **Pilot Program**: 20 African exporters on 3-month free Sokogate Platform trials (4 cohorts of 5 companies)
- **Korean Procurement Funnel**: Supplier discovery → ERS compliance dashboard → pilot transaction → corporate retainer ($1,500–$3,000/mo)
- **Revenue Target**: $22,500/mo from Korean corridor by Month 10
- **Key Partnerships**: Korean trading houses (Sangsa), Sokogate Korea corridor team
- **Full strategy**: See `KOREA-CORPORATE-STRATEGY.md`

#### 6. West Africa Expansion (Days 330–365)
- **Nigeria**: Lagos hub, partnership with Lagos Chamber of Commerce
- **Ghana**: Accra hub, partnership with Ghana Export Promotion Authority
- **Senegal**: Dakar hub, French-language platform version
