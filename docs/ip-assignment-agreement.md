# IP Assignment Agreement — RevStack / Mapato

## Parties
- **Company:** RevStack / Mapato
- **Creator:** [Employee / contractor name]

## Scope
All intellectual property created by the creator during engagement related to the company’s business (software, designs, processes, documentation).

## Key Terms
1. **Assignment** — creator assigns all rights, title, and interest in work product to the company.
2. **Moral Rights** — creator waives moral rights to the extent permitted by law.
3. **Assistance** — creator agrees to cooperate in perfecting and enforcing IP rights (including executing further documents).
4. **No Encumbrances** — creator warrants work product is original and does not infringe third-party rights.
5. **Survival** — survives termination.
