# HR Policies — RevStack / Mapato

## Policy Areas
1. **Code of Conduct** — expected behavior, harassment, discrimination.
2. **Leave Policy** — annual leave, sick leave, parental leave.
3. **Expense Policy** — reimbursable expenses, approval workflow, limits.
4. **Remote Work Policy** — eligibility, core hours, equipment.
5. **Performance Reviews** — cadence, metrics, calibration.
6. **Health & Safety** — office safety, incident reporting.
7. **IT & Security** — access controls, password rules, acceptable use.
