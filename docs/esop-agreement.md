# ESOP Agreement — RevStack / Mapato

## Plan
- Reserved pool for employees and advisors.
- Option grants are approved by the board.
- Vesting: 4 years with 1-year cliff, monthly thereafter.
- Exercise period: 90 days after termination (90-day post-termination exercise window).

## Eligibility
- Full-time employees, key contractors, and board-advisors by board resolution.

## Administration
- Option grants recorded in a separate option grant register.
- Shares issued on exercise are subject to the shareholders’ agreement.

## Status
Draft — board to approve pool size and grant policy at incorporation.
