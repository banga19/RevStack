# Offer Letters — RevStack / Mapato

## Purpose
Formal offer to a candidate before employment begins.

## Required Content
1. Candidate name and role
2. Start date and location
3. Salary/billing rate and payment schedule
4. Probationary period and duration
5. Contingencies (e.g., references, background checks)
6. Documents to bring on day 1
7. Acceptance deadline and method

## Status
Template ready for HR/legal review.
