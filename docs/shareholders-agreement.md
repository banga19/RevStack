# Shareholders’ Agreement — RevStack / Mapato

## Parties
- **Amadou Balde** — Founder / CEO
- **Bangali Fofana** — Founder / CTO
- **Option Pool** — reserved for future employees/investors

## Purpose
Governs share transfers, dividends, voting rights, and founder/investor protections.

## Key Clauses
1. **Share Classes** — ordinary shares; reserved for future preferred shares if institutional investment is raised.
2. **Voting** — ordinary resolutions by majority; reserved matters (sale, liquidation, amendment to constitution) require unanimous approval.
3. **Dividends** — no dividends until approved by board and consistent with cash needs for growth.
4. **Transfer Restrictions** — right of first refusal and co-sale rights for existing shareholders before any third-party sale.
5. **Tag-along / Drag-along** — included on founder share transfers; drag-along price floor means any buyer must offer the same terms to all shareholders.
6. **Dispute Resolution** — good-faith negotiation for 30 days, then mediation ( Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, Kenya ).
