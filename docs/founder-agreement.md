# Founder Agreement — RevStack / Mapato

## Parties
- **Amadou Balde** — CEO
- **Bangali Fofana** — CTO

## Purpose
This agreement governs the co-founding relationship of RevStack / Mapato, including roles, equity split, decision-making, and dispute resolution.

## Key Clauses
1. **Roles** — Amadou Balde leads business development, sales, partnerships, and customer success. Bangali Fofana leads engineering, architecture, security, and technical delivery.
2. **Equity Split** — to be finalized before incorporation.
3. **Decision Making** — day-to-day decisions are owned by the responsible founder; strategic decisions (fundraising, acquisition, major hires, equity changes) require unanimous consent.
4. **Time Commitment** — full-time commitment expected; neither founder may engage in competing projects without written consent.
5. **Compensation** — no salary until secured funding; operating expenses reimbursed from company funds by mutual agreement.
