# RevStack × Outplay.ai: Business Model Transformation Strategy

## Executive Summary

Transform **RevStack (Mapato)** from a B2B trade operations platform into an **AI-powered multi-channel sales engagement platform** — combining RevStack's existing trade automation strengths (WhatsApp, WATI, Instantly.ai, Hermes AI, Korea corridor) with Outplay.ai's proven playbook: built-in dialer, cold call recording, conversation intelligence, sequence builder, and SMB pricing.
