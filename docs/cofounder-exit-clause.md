# Co-founder Exit Clause — RevStack / Mapato

## Purpose
Defines what happens if a founder resigns, is removed, or cannot continue.

## Key Terms
1. **Vesting** — standard 4-year vesting with 1-year cliff. Unvested shares revert to the company.
2. **Buyout** — departing founder may sell vested shares back to the company at fair market value, determined by the board.
3. **IP Ownership** — all IP created during tenure remains company property; departing founder must assign related IP and return company property.
4. **Non-compete** — 12-month restricted period in the same market (East Africa + Korea-Africa trade corridor) unless waived by the board.
5. **Non-solicitation** — 12-month restriction on soliciting employees and customers.
