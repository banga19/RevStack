# Privacy Policy — RevStack / Mapato

## Scope
Applies to all personal data collected from users, leads, website visitors, and employees.

## Key Points
1. **Data Collected** — contact info, usage data, payment info (via Flutterwave), device/log data.
2. **Purpose** — service delivery, security, analytics, support.
3. **Legal Bases** — consent, contract performance, legitimate interests.
4. **Data Sharing** — processors and integrators (WATI, Zoho, Instantly, Voiceflow, Resend/SMTP).
5. **Data Retention** — active account duration + defined legal/business retention periods.
6. **User Rights** — access, correction, deletion, portability.
7. **Security** — encryption in transit, access controls, incident response.
8. **Contact** — support email and DPO contact (if applicable).
