# Terms of Service — RevStack / Mapato

## Applicable To
- Users of the Mapato / RevStack platform.
- Resellers or channel partners if applicable.

## Required Clauses
1. **Acceptance of Terms** — binding agreement on access/use.
2. **Service Description** — what the platform provides and does not provide.
3. **Account Responsibilities** — accurate information, security of credentials.
4. **Acceptable Use** — prohibited uses including spam, abuse, IP infringement.
5. **Fees & Payments** — subscription, taxes, refunds.
6. **Intellectual Property** — company retains platform IP; customer retains customer data ownership.
7. **Termination** — by either party; effect on data retention.
8. **Limitation of Liability** — liability caps and exclusions.
9. **Governing Law & Jurisdiction** — Kenya.
